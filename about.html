About Decathlon

Decathlon is one of the world’s largest sports retailers, operating in over 70 countries with more than 1,700 stores worldwide. Founded in France in 1976, Decathlon designs, manufactures, and sells high-quality sports equipment and apparel for over 80 sports categories.

Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)

End-to-End Sports Innovation

Decathlon controls the entire value chain — from product design to manufacturing to retail, ensuring affordability without compromising quality.

In-House Brand Portfolio

Over 60 in-house brands (like Quechua, Kipsta, Btwin, Domyos, and Kalenji) cater to specific sports, enabling deep specialization and innovation.

Affordability Through Integration

By eliminating middlemen, Decathlon offers premium products at 30–50% lower prices than competitors.

Sustainability & Responsibility

Decathlon is committed to sustainable materials, eco-design, recycling initiatives, and carbon neutrality goals.

Customer Experience Innovation

Digital-first retail, interactive in-store experiences, and advanced supply chain systems ensure convenience and consistency across all touchpoints.

Data-Driven Approach

Decathlon uses consumer insights, AI-based inventory systems, and feedback analytics to personalize offerings and improve satisfaction.
